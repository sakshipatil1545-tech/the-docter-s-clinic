About THE DOCTORS CLINIC

THE DOCTORS CLINIC in Baner, Pune, is designed as a neighborhood health center where families can walk in for routine consultations, emergency care and ongoing health management. The clinic combines accessibility, affordability and modern medical facilities to ensure that quality healthcare is available close to home. The clinic caters to patients of all age groups, with a special focus on children and family medicine. With extended evening hours up to 9:30 pm, working professionals and parents can conveniently consult the doctor after work or school.

Mission

To provide prompt, ethical and evidence-based medical care for common illnesses, emergencies and preventive health needs in a safe, friendly environment.

Vision

To be Baner’s preferred family clinic, known for trustworthy diagnosis, compassionate treatment and comprehensive basic medical services under one roof.
